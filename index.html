Why give blood?

Giving blood saves lives. The blood you give is a lifeline in an emergency and for people who need long-term treatments.

Who can give blood?

Most people can give blood. You can give blood if you / you're:

  • fit and healthy
  • weigh between 7 stone 12 lbs and 25 stone, or 50kg and 158kg
  • aged between 17 and 66 (or 70 if you have given blood before)
  • over 70 and have given a full blood donation in the last two years
